Why Is It Important To Know About Cybersecurity



From personal data to financial transactions, cyberthreats can disrupt businesses and impact individuals globally. Cybersecurity is an industry that encompasses various measures and practices that safeguard computer systems and networks from unauthorized access, damage or theft, and it involves implementing strong security protocols, complex encryption methods and proactive countermeasures.
